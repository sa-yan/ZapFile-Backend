Delete Your ZapFile Account

How to delete your ZapFile account and the data associated with it

This page applies to the ZapFile app published on Google Play. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time, using either of the methods below.

Option 1 — Delete from inside the app

  1. Open ZapFile and sign in.
  2. Scroll to the bottom of the main screen and tap Delete account.
  3. Enter your password to confirm.

Your account and data are deleted immediately.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

Email sandip@roostoo.com with the subject “Delete my ZapFile account”, sent from the email address your account is registered under. We will delete your account within 30 days and confirm by reply. Use this option if you can no longer sign in to the app.

What is deleted

DataWhat happens
Account (email address, display name, password hash)Deleted
Registered devices and push notification tokensDeleted
Friend connections and pairing codesDeleted
Transfer history (file names, sizes, timestamps, statuses)Deleted — including its appearance in your friends' history
Files saved on your or your friends' devicesNot affected — files live on the devices themselves, never on our servers

Retention

Deletion is permanent and takes effect immediately (in-app) or within 30 days (email request). We do not retain any additional copies of your personal data after deletion; encrypted infrastructure-level backups expire automatically within 30 days.

Questions

For anything else, see our privacy policy or email sandip@roostoo.com.
